Meet the transformed cryptids

- caught - actualised - guided - emboldened-


Meet Bob



Bob used to rip the flesh from hikers who had the misfortune of getting lost on the paths of The Himalayas. Terrorising villages for fun. You can see him on the right in his former form. Read about his work as our CEO and see some of his favourites on our about page.


Meet Barbara



Barbara used to snack on farmers, now she loves a tasty cracker. Her former life revolved around burning fields and eating the charred remains of the desperate workers, Barabara now works hard to help others on their journey moving from destruction to development.


Meet Barry



Barry and his brothers used to torment ships at sea, murdering thousands of men at a time purely for enjoyment (being lifelong vegetarians). Now Barry works with us on our mission. He regularly visits his brothers who live in the Scunthorpe Aquarium.
