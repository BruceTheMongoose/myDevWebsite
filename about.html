Here at Project CAGE we believe that all entities, even those spawned from the inner circles of hell still deserve the right to have a full and enjoyable life.

We practice a four step program to help these creatures terrorising communities to become an integrated part of them, working and contributing or as beautiful pets as they are best suited.
